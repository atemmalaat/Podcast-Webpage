Gold Medalist & Australian Sports Medal Recipient Joins The Searchers Podcast

Episode 43 ft. Nick Such

Nick is a highly successful coach that has been around basketball as a player and on the managerial side for decades. He has now dedicated his time to developing high performance athletes through Basketball Australia‘s Wheelchair Basketball Programs. He was a part of the men’s and women’s 2022 Commonwealth Games Campaign in Birmingham where they brought home the Gold and a Silver medal. The goal is to bring awareness to Australia’s most successful sports program, and get more coverage for these professional athletes who put in the same amount of effort as other heralded athletes. This was a great episode and we want to thank Nick for joining us and sharing his story and as well as educating us. We look forward to having Nick come back and keep up updated on their future campaigns.

About the Hosts

Atem and Kirron bring a unique blend of perspectives, drive, and passion to every episode of The Searchers Podcast. Atem, a former professional basketball player with a deep love for storytelling, channels his experiences from both the court and the classroom. He’s also a self-taught programmer, content creator, leveraging his skills in media and technology to reach audiences through YouTube and podcasting. Atem’s natural curiosity and no-nonsense approach shape the podcast’s authentic and relatable tone, making sure that each episode resonates with listeners.

Kirron, a dynamic co-host, brings his own set of insights, balancing Atem’s straightforward style with a creative flair and deep interest in people’s stories. With a background in Pschology and over 20 years of experience in coaching, he complements the podcast’s mission by helping to dig deeper into conversations that matter. Together, Atem and Kirron have built a space for open dialogue, exploration, and growth, creating a platform where diverse voices come together to share knowledge, inspiration, and a few laughs along the way.
